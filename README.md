## 🎯 What is MyAnimeMind?

MyAnimeMind isn't just another anime tracker. It's a **gamified, community-driven platform** where your watching habits earn you rewards.

Every anime you rate, every series you complete, every day you log in—you earn **Mind Tokens** to customize your profile and stand out.

**Stop googling filler lists. Stop stale recommendations. Start earning for what you already love doing.**

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎭 Mood Matcher
Tell the AI how you feel—sad, hyped, curious, romantic—and get instant anime recommendations tailored to your exact mood. Pick your vibe, how much time you have, and any dealbreakers.

### 🟢🔴 Filler Detector
Color-coded episodes so you never waste time on filler again:
- 🟢 **Green** = Canon (essential story)
- 🟡 **Yellow** = Mixed (some story, some filler)
- 🔴 **Red** = Filler (completely skipable)

Click "Skip to Next Canon" and jump straight to the good stuff.

### 💰 Token Economy
Earn Mind Tokens for:
- ✅ Daily login (5 tokens)
- ✅ Rating an anime (2 tokens)
- ✅ Completing a series (10 tokens)
- ✅ Writing a review (15 tokens)
- ✅ Referring friends (50 tokens)

### 🎨 Customizable Profiles
Spend your tokens on:
- Custom profile banners
- Animated avatars
- Special profile borders
- Custom theme colors
- Exclusive badges

### 📋 Public Lists
Create and share curated anime lists with the community.

### 💀 Drop Reasons
Anonymously share why you dropped a show. Help others avoid the same frustrations.

### 📊 Smart Stats
See your total anime watched, episodes, time spent (in hours), favorite genre, favorite studio, and highest-rated anime.
